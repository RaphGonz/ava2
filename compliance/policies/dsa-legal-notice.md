# EU Digital Services Act (DSA) Legal Notice

**Status:** LIVE
**Effective Date:** 2026-02-24

---

## 1. Single Point of Contact (Articles 11 & 12)

In compliance with the Digital Services Act (Regulation (EU) 2022/2065), we have designated a single point of contact for:
1.  **Member State Authorities, the EU Commission, and the Board:**
    * Email: legal-eu@ava-platform.com
    * Languages supported: English, French
2.  **Recipients of the Service (Users):**
    * Email: support-eu@ava-platform.com
    * Communication via: In-app support ticket system

## 2. Illegal Content Reporting (Article 16)

Any individual or entity may report content they consider illegal under EU or national laws.
* **Reporting Mechanism:** Use the "Report" button available on every chat or image, or email moderation@ava-platform.com with the subject "DSA Notice".
* **Required Information:**
    * Explanation of why the content is illegal.
    * Exact location (URL or Message ID).
    * Name and email address of the reporter (except for offenses related to sexual abuse of children).
    * Statement of good faith.

## 3. Statement on Recommender Systems (Article 27)

Our AI companion uses algorithmic systems to generate responses and suggest conversation topics.
* **Main Parameters:** The AI prioritizes context relevance, user-defined persona settings, and historical conversation continuity.
* **User Control:** Users can modify these parameters by adjusting the "Spice Level" and "Persona Settings" in their account dashboard.

## 4. Internal Complaint-Handling System (Article 20)

If we remove content or suspend your account, you have the right to appeal this decision for a period of **6 months**.
* **How to Appeal:** Reply to the moderation notification or contact support@ava-platform.com with your Case ID.
* **Review:** Appeals are reviewed by a human moderator, not an automated system.

## 5. Out-of-Court Dispute Settlement (Article 21)

If the internal appeal fails, users residing in the EU are entitled to select a certified out-of-court dispute settlement body to resolve the dispute.
