## 2. Takedown Notice Requirements

If you believe your copyrighted work has been infringed on our platform, you must submit a written notification including:
1.  Physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or authorized representative.
2.  Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed.
3.  Identification of the material that is claimed to be infringing (URL or Image ID).
4.  Contact information (Address, Email, Phone).
5.  A statement that you have a "good faith belief" that the use is not authorized.
6.  A statement that the information is accurate and, under penalty of perjury, that you are authorized to act.

## 3. Counter-Notification

If you believe your content was removed by mistake, you may file a counter-notification containing:
1.  Your physical or electronic signature.
2.  Identification of the material that has been removed.
3.  A statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief the removal was a result of mistake or misidentification.
4.  Your name, address, and telephone number, and consent to the jurisdiction of the Federal District Court.
