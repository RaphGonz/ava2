## Exemption Statement regarding Record-Keeping Requirements

The "Ava" platform and all content hosted therein are **exempt** from the record-keeping requirements of 18 U.S.C. § 2257 and 28 C.F.R. § 75 because:

1.  **100% Computer-Generated:** All visual depictions available on this platform are created entirely by Artificial Intelligence (computer-generated imagery).
2.  **No Real Persons:** The content does not depict any actual human being, real person, or minor. Any resemblance to actual persons, living or dead, is purely coincidental.
3.  **Fictional Characters:** All "personas" and "avatars" are fictional entities created via text descriptions and algorithmic generation.

As such, no "secondary producer" records are required to be maintained, as the content falls outside the scope of "visually depicting a significantly sexual activity" involving a "performer" (real human).
