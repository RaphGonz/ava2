<task type="checkpoint:human-verify">
  <name>Task 2: Email DNS verification via mail-tester.com</name>
  <what-built>
DNS records were added in Plan 02. By now (24+ hours later), they should have propagated. This task verifies inbox deliverability scores 9/10 or higher.

Note: If you are running this plan within a few hours of Plan 02, DNS records may not have propagated yet. In that case, skip this task and return to it after 24 hours. The phase is not complete until mail-tester.com passes.
  </what-built>
  <how-to-verify>
STEP 1 — Check DNS propagation:
```bash
# Replace resend._domainkey.yourdomain.com with your actual domain
dig TXT resend._domainkey.yourdomain.com +short
# If empty: DNS has not propagated yet — wait and retry later
# If returns a long TXT value: propagation complete
```

STEP 2 — Verify domain in Resend Dashboard:
- Go to resend.com/domains
- Your domain should show green checkmarks next to SPF, DKIM, and MX
- If still showing "Pending" after 48 hours: check you added the records to the correct subdomain

STEP 3 — Send mail-tester.com test email:
- Go to https://mail-tester.com
- Copy the unique test email address shown (format: test-xxxxx@mail-tester.com)
- In Resend Dashboard → Emails → Send Test Email:
  - From: your configured From address (e.g. ava@yourdomain.com)
  - To: the mail-tester.com unique address
  - Subject: Test
  - Click Send
- Return to mail-tester.com and click "Check your score"

STEP 4 — Review results:
- Score must be 9/10 or higher (ROADMAP.md Phase 8 success criteria #5)
- Common deductions:
  - Missing DMARC: add _dmarc TXT record
  - SPF not aligned: ensure sending from the domain you configured, not a subdomain without SPF
  - DKIM invalid: re-copy the key from Resend Dashboard (it's sensitive to whitespace)

STOP SIGNAL: If score is below 9/10, do NOT proceed to Phase 9. Diagnose the failing checks shown on mail-tester.com and fix them before declaring Phase 8 complete.
